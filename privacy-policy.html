Privacy Policy - Space Scape

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Space Scape ("we", "our", or "the game") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Data: Progress, scores, achievements, and statistics are stored locally on your device.
  • Ad Data: Through Google AdMob, data may be collected for ad personalization (subject to your consent).
  • Google Play Games: If you choose to connect, achievement and leaderboard data is synced.
  • Firebase Analytics (with consent): Anonymous gameplay data such as distance traveled, ship used, upgrade level, and session duration, used to improve game balance. Collection is only enabled with your explicit consent.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Automatic error reports containing technical device information (model, OS version, stack trace) for bug fixing. Contains no personally identifiable data and is always active to ensure app stability.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts
  • We do not track your GPS location
  • We do not share your data with third parties beyond the services mentioned herein

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Save your game progress locally
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (per your consent)
  • Sync achievements and leaderboards with Google Play Games (optional)
  • Analyze anonymous gameplay data to improve game balance and experience (per your consent)
  • Identify and fix technical errors and crashes in the application

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data according to its own privacy policy. You can choose to receive personalized or non-personalized ads through the in-game consent dialog. For more information:
Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Play Games

If you choose to connect to Google Play Games, your achievements and scores will be synced according to the Google Play Games policy.

4.3 Google Play Billing

We use Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases and to deliver pre-registration rewards to eligible users. Purchase data is handled by Google according to their privacy policy. For more information:
Google Privacy Policy

4.4 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay data and Firebase Crashlytics for technical error reporting. Analytics data collection is only enabled with your explicit consent. Crashlytics collects only non-personal technical data (device model, OS version, and error information) and is always active to ensure app stability. For more information:
Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

All game data (progress, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your device using localStorage. This data is not sent to external servers, except:

  • Achievements and leaderboards synced with Google Play Games (if connected)
  • Anonymous ad usage data through AdMob
  • Anonymous gameplay data sent to Firebase Analytics (if consented)
  • Technical error reports sent to Firebase Crashlytics

6. Security

We implement security measures to protect your locally stored data. However, no method of storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the application at any time
  • Clear game data through your device settings
  • Disconnect from Google Play Games
  • Manage your privacy preferences (analytics and personalized ads) directly in the game under Settings > Manage Privacy
  • Opt out of personalized ads through Google's settings
  • Purchase the premium version to remove ads

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through an in-app update or notice on the home screen.

10. Consent

When you first launch Space Scape, you will be asked to give explicit consent for analytics data collection and for the display of personalized advertisements. You can change your preferences at any time under Settings > Manage Privacy.
